01 On the name

In every Thai kitchen, there is one dish that needs no decision. You walk into a food stall tired, hungry, talking on the phone — and you order it without thinking. กะเพราไข่ดาว. Pad krapow with a fried egg over rice. It is the country's quiet default; the closest thing Thailand has to a national reflex.

We chose the name because we want our software to feel the same way. Familiar. Unfussy. The thing your hand reaches for before your brain finishes the sentence. A small comfort, repeated daily, that asks nothing back.

02 What we make

Software, in our preferred Thai expression, that is โคตร easy — stupidly easy. Tools you don't have to learn. Apps that get out of the way once they've done what you asked. We start from the verb, not the feature list.

Most software earns its complexity. Some software earns its simplicity. We are interested in the second kind. If a thing we ship does not pass the stupid-easy test, we don't ship it.
